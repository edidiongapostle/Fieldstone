Our Story

Built on solid ground since 1987.

We started as one branch in a small Ohio town with a simple idea: banking works best when decisions are made by people who actually know you. Almost forty years later, that's still how we operate.

How We Started

A bank that started with a "no."

In 1987, a handful of shop owners in Fieldstone, Ohio kept hearing the same answer from the big banks in the city: their small-business loans were too small to bother with. So they pooled their savings, hired eleven employees, and opened Fieldstone Savings & Loan on Main Street.

The bet was simple — if you treat every customer's money like it's your own neighbor's, people notice. It worked. Fieldstone grew branch by branch, county by county, without ever losing the habit of having a real person review every loan.

Today we're a full-service national bank with more than 850 branches, but the name on the door still points back to that first Main Street address.
